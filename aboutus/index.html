Dr ravisankar children hospital is a state of the art children hospital in madurai , fully equipped to handle all kinds of paediatric emergencies and ailments related to children. From childhood vaccination, nebulization to all paediatric emergencies, we have handled every ailment of children with utmost care, that we have evolved as the best child specialist in Madurai.

Our centre is the paediatric referral centre both for admissions and emergencies for most of the hospitals in madurai and outside madurai. We have a well established NICU , department paediatric pulmonogist , department of paediatric nephrology , paediatric gastroenterology and paediatric neurology . We have 24 hrs on call paediatrician , recident child specialist , and visiting constants.

We have now evolved as a 24 hrs children hospital with admission facilities, 24 hrs outpatient management facility, cashless insurance facility, star health insurance empaneled hospital, Mediclaim insurance facility, 24 hrs pharmacy, laboratory facility, childhood asthma, and wheezing treatment center, Neonatal ICU and paediatric ICU facility.
